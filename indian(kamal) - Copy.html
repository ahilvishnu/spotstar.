""Indian" (1996) is an Indian Tamil-language film starring Kamal Haasan. The story revolves around Senapathy, a freedom fighter turned vigilante, who takes on corruption and injustice in society. Disillusioned by the system's failure, Senapathy becomes a ruthless killer targeting corrupt officials. His dual role as a grandfather and grandson adds complexity to the narrative. The film explores themes of patriotism, justice, and the impact of corruption on society. With intense performances by Kamal Haasan, "Indian" remains a powerful commentary on societal issues, blending action and drama to deliver a compelling tale of one man's fight against the system.
