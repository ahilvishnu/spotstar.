Rajinikanth, a legendary Tamil actor, is an iconic figure in Indian cinema. Born on December 12, 1950, as Shivaji Rao Gaekwad, he rose to fame with his unique style, charismatic screen presence, and versatile performances. Fondly known as "Thalaivar" (leader), Rajinikanth has a massive fan following, transcending regional boundaries. His career spans over four decades, featuring blockbuster films like "Muthu," "Sivaji," and "Kabali." Beyond acting, he is revered for his humility, philanthropy, and for entering politics in later years. Rajinikanth's larger-than-life persona and his impact on the film industry have solidified his status as a cultural icon.
