The Kamal Haasan starrer Vikram, directed by Lokesh Kanagaraj, revolves around a gripping narrative. The story follows Vikram, a highly skilled and enigmatic police officer, as he navigates through a complex web of crime and corruption. Unraveling a series of mysteries, Vikram confronts formidable adversaries, showcasing his intellect and physical prowess. The film combines intense action sequences with a thought-provoking plot, keeping the audience on the edge of their seats. Lokesh Kanagaraj's directorial prowess and Kamal Haasan's commanding performance promise a compelling cinematic experience, making Vikram a highly anticipated and engaging film for fans and cinephiles alike.
