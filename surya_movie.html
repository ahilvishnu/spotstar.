Suriya, an acclaimed Tamil actor, born on July 23, 1975, in Chennai, India, is a versatile performer known for his dynamic screen presence and acting prowess. With a career spanning over two decades, he has delivered numerous blockbuster films, showcasing his ability to excel in diverse roles, from action-packed ventures to poignant dramas. Suriya is not only a celebrated actor but also a philanthropist, actively involved in social causes through his Agaram Foundation. His dedication to both his craft and humanitarian efforts has earned him a dedicated fan base and widespread recognition, solidifying his status as a prominent figure in the Tamil film industry.
