Vijay, a charismatic and versatile Indian actor, began his acting career with the film "Naalaiya Theerpu" in 1992, showcasing his exceptional talent from the start. Over the years, he has become a beloved figure in the South Indian film industry, starring in over 65 films. With a significant fan following, Vijay is known for his dynamic performances, seamlessly transitioning between action, romance, and drama genres. His notable movies include "Ghilli," "Mersal," and "Master," solidifying his status as a leading actor. Vijay's on-screen charisma, coupled with his philanthropic efforts off-screen, has endeared him to audiences worldwide.
