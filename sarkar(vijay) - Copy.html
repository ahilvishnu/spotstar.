"Sarkar" is a 2018 Tamil political thriller film starring Vijay. The story revolves around Sundar Ramasamy (Vijay), a successful businessman in the USA, who returns to India to cast his vote. However, he discovers rampant corruption and political malpractices. When his efforts to bring about change through legal means fail, he takes matters into his own hands, using his business acumen and intelligence to confront the corrupt political system. As he challenges the powerful, the film explores themes of politics, corruption, and the consequences of one man's fight against a deeply entrenched system.
