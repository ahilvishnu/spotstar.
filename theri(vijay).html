"Theri" is a Tamil action film directed by Atlee. The story revolves around Joseph Kuruvilla, played by Vijay, a doting father who conceals his violent past. After relocating with his daughter to lead a peaceful life, circumstances force him to confront his history as a cop. Unveiling a revenge plot, Joseph transforms into a fearless avenger against societal injustice. The film blends intense action, emotional drama, and a strong social message, showcasing Vijay's versatility. With engaging performances and impactful storytelling, "Theri" is a gripping tale of retribution and redemption in the face of personal and social challenges.
