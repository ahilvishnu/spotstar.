"Soorarai Pottru" is a Tamil film based on the life of Captain G. R. Gopinath, the founder of Air Deccan. Nedumaaran Rajangam, inspired by his father's dream, strives to make air travel accessible to the common man. Facing numerous obstacles, he battles against a corrupt system and powerful adversaries. The film highlights perseverance, entrepreneurship, and the indomitable spirit of a man determined to realize his vision against all odds. With compelling performances, it showcases the journey of a relentless dreamer who transforms challenges into opportunities in the pursuit of making aviation affordable for everyone.
