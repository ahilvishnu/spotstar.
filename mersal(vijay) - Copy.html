"Mersal" is a 2017 Indian Tamil-language action thriller film directed by Atlee. The story revolves around three identical brothers, Vetri, Maaran, and Vetrimaaran, played by Vijay. Dr. Maaran, a renowned doctor, uncovers a medical scam that connects him to his lookalikes and a powerful politician. As the brothers seek justice, they unravel a complex conspiracy involving illegal organ trade and corruption. The film seamlessly weaves together action, drama, and social commentary, showcasing the lead actor's versatility. With impactful performances, engaging plot twists, and a strong social message, "Mersal" became a blockbuster hit and received critical acclaim.
