"Dheena" is a Tamil action film directed by A. R. Murugadoss. The story revolves around Dheena, portrayed by Ajith Kumar, a former militant who seeks redemption. After being presumed dead, Dheena lives an undercover life to protect a family from political enemies. His past catches up with him when his identity is exposed. With his unparalleled combat skills, Dheena must confront powerful adversaries to safeguard the family. The film explores themes of loyalty, sacrifice, and the consequences of one's past actions. Action-packed sequences and emotional moments drive the narrative, making "Dheena" a compelling tale of redemption and personal sacrifice.
