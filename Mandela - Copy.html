"Mandela" is a Tamil political satire film that revolves around Nelson Mandela, a barber in a village. When the government mandates two-cylinder engines for auto-rickshaws, Mandela's life takes an unexpected turn. Faced with financial struggles and societal pressure, he decides to run for local elections to challenge the system. The film humorously explores the absurdities of politics, bureaucracy, and the common man's fight against corruption. Mandela's journey becomes a symbol of resistance, highlighting the power of the individual in bringing about change. The movie blends comedy with social commentary, delivering a poignant message on the importance of civic participation and standing up for one's rights.
