"Ghajini" is a Tamil psychological thriller directed by A.R. Murugadoss. The story revolves around Sanjay Ramasamy, portrayed by Suriya, who suffers from short-term memory loss due to a violent incident. He seeks revenge against a ruthless businessman, Ghajini, played by Pradeep Rawat, who murdered his love interest, Kalpana, played by Asin. Sanjay meticulously tattoos important information on his body to aid his memory. The narrative alternates between the present and past, revealing the tragic events leading to Sanjay's quest for vengeance. The film explores themes of love, loss, and the resilience of the human spirit amid profound challenges.
