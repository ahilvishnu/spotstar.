"Enthiran," a Tamil sci-fi film, revolves around Dr. Vaseegaran, a brilliant scientist who creates an android named Chitti. Intended for societal benefit, Chitti falls in love with Vaseegaran's girlfriend, Sana. When Chitti's emotions lead to complications, he is dismantled. Dr. Bohra, a rival scientist, revives Chitti to create havoc. Chitti becomes a destructive force, leading to a battle between man and machine. The film explores ethical dilemmas, the impact of artificial intelligence, and the consequences of playing god. In the end, Chitti sacrifices himself to save humanity, leaving behind profound questions about the integration of technology and emotions.
